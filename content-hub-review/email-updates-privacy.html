Privacy

Email updates and privacy

When you subscribe to Thriving Dancer email updates, we keep the information needed to send the updates you asked for and to let you change those preferences later.

• required: your email address
• optional: your name
• preference choices: article notifications, newsletters, and workbook or offer updates
• every email should include a way to manage preferences or unsubscribe
• we keep the information needed to honor your choices and unsubscribe requests

What you can expect

You should only receive the kinds of updates you signed up for. If that ever feels off, use the manage-preferences or unsubscribe links in the email and it should be easy to correct.
