Picking Up Choreography After Years of Salsa

4 min readMedia versions planned

If you've done some dance before picking up the basics of the choreography, wasn't it too bad, I think I'm mostly got it. So I'm pretty good at picking up choreography and then I'll watch dance moves on YouTube and copy them. I have been doing salsa for the past 10-plus years.

<p>This short clip explores how previous dance experience impacts the speed of learning new choreography, particularly when someone already has years of salsa and a habit of studying movement by watching and repeating.</p>
