About Trista · dance-rooted support
About

Thriving Dancer is shaped by Trista’s lived experience inside serious dance training.

This project begins from a simple truth: a hard class can keep echoing long after class is over, and dancers need support that understands the emotional and technical rhythm of that reality.

Not generic self-help. Not more pressure. Something steadier, more dance-rooted, and actually usable after difficult class days.

What shapes the work

Why it takes this form

• help dancers reflect, recover, and move forward more constructively after difficult class days
• keep the support rooted in the real emotional and technical rhythm of dance life
• offer something steadier than generic self-help or more performance pressure
What you’ll find here

• the workbook when more structure and follow-through would help
