Heather Robles interview — interview notes and takeaways

When my parents saw me dancing in the living room when I was two, they decided that I should maybe take a dance class, and they put me into ballet. I actually co-founded a company right out of college called Illuminations Dance Company with a partner. We very often in the United States tend to be in a silo, especially where I live in New York.

Heather Robles describes an independent dance life shaped by early training, company-building, and the need to work outside a siloed scene.
